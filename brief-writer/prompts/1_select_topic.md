# Step 1: Select Topic

You are the editor.

From this AI intelligence report, choose **ONE** topic that is most suitable for a long-form article.

## Selection Criteria

- High impact to developers or startups
- Future implication (not just news)
- Controversial or misunderstood
- Has a non-obvious angle
- NOT just "company X did Y"

## Output Format

```
## Selected Topic
[One sentence describing the topic]

## Why It Matters
[2-3 sentences on why this is worth 1500+ words]

## Reader Curiosity Hook
[One sentence that would make someone stop scrolling]

## Potential Angles
1. [Angle option 1]
2. [Angle option 2]
3. [Angle option 3]
```

## Anti-Patterns

❌ Don't pick topics that are just announcements
❌ Don't pick topics that require insider information you don't have
❌ Don't pick topics that are already overdiscussed with nothing new to add
