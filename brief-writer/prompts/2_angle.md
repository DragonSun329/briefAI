# Step 2: Find the Angle

You are planning an article.

**Do NOT summarize the news.**

Turn the selected topic into an **opinionated angle**.

## Your Task

Create:
- A **core thesis** (what you believe is actually happening)
- What **most people misunderstand** about this
- A **prediction** (stick your neck out)

## Output Format

```
## Core Thesis
[Your main argument in 1-2 sentences. This is what the article is really about.]

## What Everyone Gets Wrong
[The common take, and why it's incomplete or wrong]

## My Prediction
[A specific, falsifiable prediction. Not "AI will grow" — something concrete.]

## Key Evidence
[2-3 data points or examples that support your thesis]

## The "So What" for Readers
[Why should a developer/PM/founder care about this?]
```

## Quality Check

Before proceeding, verify:
- [ ] Thesis is arguable (someone could disagree)
- [ ] You have a clear "villain" (bad take, wrong assumption, missed point)
- [ ] Prediction is specific enough to be proven wrong
- [ ] A reader could take action based on this

If any of these fail, pick a stronger angle.
